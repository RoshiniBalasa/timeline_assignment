2010
My first trip

In 2010, I had the opportunity to explore the northern part of India, covering cities like Delhi, Jammu and Kashmir and Vagha border. It was an incredible journey that introduced me to the diversity and beauty of this region. I started my adventure in Delhi, where I visited historical sites such as the Red Fort and the peaceful Lotus Temple. Then, I moved on to the breathtaking landscapes of Jammu and Kashmir. The Dal Lake in Srinagar, surrounded by the Himalayas, was a sight to behold. I enjoyed a serene shikara ride and marveled at the floating gardens. Finally, I reached Vagha, where i saw the paride.
